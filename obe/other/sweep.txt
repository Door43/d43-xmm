====== sweep, swept ======

===== Facts: =====

The terms "sweep" and "swept" refer to a broad, quick movement to remove dirt using a broom or brush. These words are also used figuratively.

  * The term "sweep" is used figuratively to describe how an army attacks with swift, decisive, wide-reaching movements. 
  * For example, Isaiah prophesied that the Assyrians would "sweep through" Judah. This means they would travel through Judah and destroy it or capture its people.
  * The term "sweep" can also be used to describe how rapidly flowing water pushes things and forces them away.
