====== on high ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "on high" is a figurative way of referring to where God lives.

  * Also an idiomatic expression meaning to be exalted or lifted up. Psalm 97 says "you are the Lord Most High over all the earth; You are exalted far above all gods."
  * The word "high" indicates the importance of a location or person.
  * At the birth of Christ, the heavenly hosts sang, "Glory to God in the highest", "highest" meaning heaven.

(See also: other)
