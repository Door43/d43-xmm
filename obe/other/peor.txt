====== Peor, Mount Peor, Baal Peor ======

===== Facts: =====
Peor was the name of a mountain in the land where the tribe of Reuben lived. 

  * Peor or Beth Peor was also a city located in the region of Moab, which was east of the Salt Sea.
  * Baal Peor was a false god of the Moabites that was worshiped at Mount Peor.
