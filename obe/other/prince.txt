====== prince ======

===== Definition: =====

The term "prince" literally refers to the son of a king. However, this word is often used as a general reference to a leader, ruler or other powerful person.

  * Because of Abraham's wealth and importance, he was referred to as a "prince" by the Hittites he was living among.
  * Jesus is called the Prince of Peace and the Prince of Life.
  * In Acts 2:36, Jesus is referred to as "Lord and Christ" and in Acts 5:31 he is referred to as "Prince and Savior"; this indicates that in this context "Prince" is a synonym for "Lord."
  * In the book of Daniel, the term "prince" is in used the expressions "prince of Persia" and "prince of Greece," which many believe refer to evil spirits who had authority or power over those regions. The archangel Michael is also referred to as a "prince" in this book.
  * The term "princess" refers to a daughter of a king.

Translation Suggestions:

  * Depending on the context, possible ways to translate this term could include, "king's son, ruler, leader, chieftain, or captain."
  * When referring to angels or evil spirits, this could also be translated as, "spirit ruler" or "angel" or "evil spirit ruler" or "powerful spirit lord," depending on the context.
