====== teach, teaching, instruct, instruction ======

===== Definition: =====
The terms "teach" and "teaching" both mean to tell other people information they didn't know before. Usually these terms refer to giving information in a formal or systematic way.

  * A teacher is someone who teaches. 
  * The information given could be shown or spoken.
  * In the Bible, teaching is typically a new piece of information that was unknown before.
  * Teaching is also translated as "doctrine" sometimes when it refers to a set of teachings, especially about God.
