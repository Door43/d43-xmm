====== precious ======

===== Facts: =====

Things which are valuable, rare or expensive.

  * Jewels, of which there are many kinds and colors, and gold are often spoken of as precious in the Old Testament.
  * The lovingkindness of the Lord is described as being precious.
  * The Lord God calls Israel "precious in My sight" in Isaiah 43:4.
  * Peter refers to the "hidden person of the heart" being precious in the sight of God. 1 Peter 3:4

(See also: other)
