====== Phinehas ======

===== Facts: =====


Phineas was one of Aaron's grandsons. He was a Levitical priest who was faithfully devoted to Yahweh.

  * Phinehas saved the Israelites from a plague that Yahweh had sent to punish them for marrying pagan women and worshiping their false gods.
  * He killed a man who had disobeyed God by bringing into his tent a Midianite woman, which God had forbidden the Israelites to do.
  * Phinehas was overseer of the gatekeepers and on several occasions accompanied armed forces to destroy the Midianites.
  * He was the leader of a group of ten leaders who went to tribes east of the Jordan River to rebuke them for building an altar for a false god.
