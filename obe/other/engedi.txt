====== Engedi ======

===== Definition: =====
Engedi is a city with a fountain of water uses as a place of rest in the wilderness of Judah. 

  * Engedi is on the western bank of the Dead Sea. 
  * David fled to Engedi when he was being chased by King Saul.
  * Engedi was known for having beautiful vineyards.
