====== commit, committed, commitment ======

===== Definition: =====
The terms "commit" and "commitment" refer to making a promise or decision to do something and then doing it.

  * The term "committed" describes someone who has decided to do what they have promised to do. It means they are "faithful" or "devoted" to do that task.
  * Another meaning of "commit" means to do a certain wrong action such as "commit a sin" or "commit adultery" or "commit murder."
  * The term "commit" and "committed" can also mean "to be consecrated" or "to be given" for a certain task.
  * A task that is committed to someone is "given" or "entrusted" to them to do.
