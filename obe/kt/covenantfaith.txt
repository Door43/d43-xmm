====== covenant faithfulness ======

===== Definition: =====

This term is used to describe God's commitment to fulfill his promises that he made to his people.

  * God made promises to the Israelites in formal agreements called "covenants."
  * God keeps his promises to His people.
  * One way that God demonstrates his grace to his people is his faithfulness to his covenant promises.
