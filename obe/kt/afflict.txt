====== afflict, affliction ======

===== Definition: =====

To "afflict" is to cause someone mental or physical distress or suffering. An "affliction" is the disease, emotional grief, or other disaster that results from this.

  * God afflicted his people with sickness or other hardships in order to cause them to repent of their sins and turn back to him.
  * God caused afflictions to come on the people of Egypt because their king refused to obey God.


===== Translation Suggestions: =====

  * To afflict someone could be translated as "cause someone to experience difficulty" or "cause someone to suffer" or "cause suffering to come."
  * A phrase like, "afflict someone with leprosy" could be translated as, "cause someone to be sick with leprosy."
  * Depending on the context, the term "affliction" could be translated as "calamity" or "sickness" or "suffering" or "great distress."
